### Design Decisions

- Alert handler on timer thread; VM marshals via BeginInvoke (non-blocking)
- Tray color driven by OnTimerTick only (no redundant state changes from OnAlertRaised)
- Pulse via event + storyboard (guaranteed to replay on rapid alerts, unlike bool DataTrigger)
- PulseFlash sibling of RootBorder (independent of existing border hierarchy)
- Alert message injected into StackPanel (not separate window; respects SizeToContent)
- SoundAlertService recreated per-session with fresh AlertSettings
- Focus protection: no Activate/Focus calls; balloon tips inherently non-focus-stealing
